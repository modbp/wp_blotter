Blog comments powered by Twitter

== Description ==

Blotter revolutionizes blog commenting. No more empty comment sections or, even worse, spam-filled rants. With Blotter, your blog comments can drive honest, authentic conversation with your readers.

You can learn more about how Blotter works at https://useblotter.com.


== Installation ==

1. Go to `Plugins` in the Admin menu
2. Click on the button `Add new`
3. Search for `Blotter` and click 'Install Now' or click on the `upload` link to upload `blotter.zip`
4. Click on `Activate plugin`
5. On your WordPress admin dashboard, navigate to Comments > Blotter. Insert your Blotter Host ID from your Blotter dashboard and hit save.
6. Thatâ€™s it, your Blotter comments are now live! Follow the instructions on your Blotter dashboard or go to one of your blog posts to start more conversations with your readers.
